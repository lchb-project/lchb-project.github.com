News 06/02/2026

Rakennusterveys.net's IEQ Virtual Lab is now opened!

The IEQ Virtual Lab is a hub for digital tools, datasets, and databases that support the exploration and analysis of indoor environmental quality. It is designed for students, researchers, and practitioners, and this page aggregates links to related projects.

This platform will include a comprehensive set of tools designed to analyse IEQ data from various projects, including indoor data and KPIs, occupancy rates, building data, outdoor air quality and weather data. More tools will be available. If you have or know a project that could belong there, please let us know at rakennusterveys@oulu.fi.
